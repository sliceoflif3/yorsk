This part of the document contains the written works that relate to the story behind the songs of Yorushika. Their arrangements are as-is from their original translations and may differ from other translations and from the original source text from Yorushika.

There are currently 3 main branches of story, and as of the time of this writing (02/09/2023) it is confirmed through interviews with n-buna, and through the Lives, that ALL of the works of Yorushika are inter-connected.

The names of the branches are not official.
